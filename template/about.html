ABOUT US

Welcome to Modern Bahay Kubo, your gateway to the harmonious fusion of traditional Filipino architecture and contemporary design: the modern Bahay Kubo. Our mission is to celebrate the enduring legacy of the Bahay Kubo while exploring its exciting evolution in the 21st century.

We believe that the Bahay Kubo's inherent principles of sustainability, adaptability, and community are more relevant than ever. Our team is dedicated to showcasing how these timeless values can be seamlessly integrated with modern materials, innovative technologies, and contemporary aesthetics.

We offer a wealth of resources and inspiration, including:

  • Design Showcases: Explore stunning examples of modern Bahay Kubo designs, featuring innovative architectural solutions and sustainable materials.
  • Historical Insights: Delve into the rich history and cultural significance of the traditional Bahay Kubo.
  • Architect and Designer Profiles: Discover the talented individuals who are shaping the future of Filipino architecture.

Our goal is to inspire a new generation of homeowners and architects to embrace the beauty and practicality of the modern Bahay Kubo, creating homes that are both culturally rich and environmentally responsible. Join us as we reimagine the Filipino home for the future.
